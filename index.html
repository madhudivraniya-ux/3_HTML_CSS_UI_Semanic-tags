web design

Web Design is the process of creating and arranging visual elements, layout, and content on a website to enhance user experience and achieve specific goals.
Web design is a discipline that involves planning, conceptualizing, and designing digital interfaces for websites.
It combines visual design principles, user experience strategies, and responsive techniques to create websites that are visually appealing, functional, and accessible across different devices and screen sizes.

ui ux

User Interface is a field of design concerned with the look and feel of the product or a web page.
It deals with the layout of the product presented to the customer or user.
On the other hand, user experience is concerned with providing real and meaningful experiences to the user.
It is much broader in scope and includes the usability, aesthetics, and functionality of the product.
